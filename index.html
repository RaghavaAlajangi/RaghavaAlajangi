Beyond ML, I contribute to the core open-source libraries used in our lab (dclab, shapeout2, dcor), maintain Docker-based CI pipelines, and serve as the team’s IT and software lead—resolving issues, implementing features, and ensuring high-quality development practices.

I am passionate about building systems that combine AI, automation, and reproducibility—especially in healthcare and research environments. My goal is to continue bridging the gap between ML research and production deployment in impactful domains.
